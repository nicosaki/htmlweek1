Weird Animals Sans Skeletons

Invertebrates are neat. They don't have traditional endoskeletons like mammals or other vertebrates.

Some obscure invertebrate phylums and their common names

  • Onychophora - "Velvet Worms"
  • Rotifera - "Rotifers"
  • Nematoda - "Nematodes"
  • Ctenophora - "Comb Jellies"
  • Tardigrada - "Water Bears"
